Our Story

Founded in 2025 by Kim, Kim's Antiques began as a passion project born from countless hours spent wandering through flea markets and antique shops across the globe. What started as a personal fascination with historical artifacts and vintage treasures has evolved into a curated marketplace for discerning collectors and vintage enthusiasts.

Meet Kim

Kim

Founder & Chief Curator

With over a decade of experience in antiques and collectibles, Kim has developed an eye for exceptional pieces that tell compelling stories. Her travels have taken her to markets across Europe, Asia, and the Americas, building relationships with dealers and artisans who share her passion for preserving history.

Kim's expertise spans furniture, decorative arts, jewelry, and rare collectibles. She believes that antiques should be lived with and enjoyed, not merely displayed, and she takes pride in helping customers find pieces that will become treasured parts of their lives.
